The Sound of the Far Future

A computer performance of ambient music about events that will happen trillions of years from now and beyond.
  • Turn on your sound.
  • Once you start the events, you can drag the timeline up and down to move time forward and backward, but time will start moving on its own after you've let go for a bit.
  • You don't have to keep looking at this page. You can put the browser in the background, and it'll keep playing and informing you about the far future as you go on about your day.
  • For listening convenience, stream or download a recording at Bandcamp.
Your device doesn't have the Web Audio support we need to perform live. Fortunately, you can just listen to the album on Bandcamp!
